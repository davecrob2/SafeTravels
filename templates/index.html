About Me.

My name is David Robinson and I graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a Bachelor's of Science in Accountancy. I am a Certified Public Accountant (CPA), working as an External Auditor for a Big 4 Public Accounting Firm.

I have been interested in technology since I was a teenager, developing basic HTML and Javascript skills. In March of 2017, I committed to becoming proficient in Python as a hobby and to develop a diverse skillset. The financial services industry has begun to invest heavily in technology efforts to offer more forward-thinking services to their clients. As a result, I saw this as the perfect opportunity to get ahead of the curve.

Currently, I am working on a few side projects as I learn different Pythom libraries. First, I am developing a Python based Enterprise Resource Planning System (ERP from the ground up using a Tkinter GUI and SQLite database. My eventual goal is to implement this ERP system via a web interface such as Django or Flask, however I am still in the infancy phase of the project. I undertook this project due to my experience working with client's ERP system outputs. One of my guiding principles for this project is to make an efficient, "packagable", audit-focused ERP system that provides the functionality for the client to run their business while reducing response time for year-end audits.
